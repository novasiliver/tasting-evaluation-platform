Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about the submission process.

How many samples do I need to send?

We require a minimum of 6 units for standard evaluation. Additional units may be needed for products with variations or for detailed lab analysis.

Can I submit multiple products at once?

Yes! We offer discounts for multi-product submissions. Submit each product separately and reference your other submissions for the discount.

What if my product requires refrigeration?

Note this in the handling instructions. We'll provide specific shipping guidance to ensure your product arrives in optimal condition.

Are my results confidential?

Yes, all evaluation results are completely confidential unless you choose to publicize them or qualify for and accept an award.
